The NextGen Story

Two brothers, a lifetime of tradition, and a commitment to first-class craftsmanship.

We didn't just pick up a brush five years ago; we were born into the trade. Growing up as second-generation painters, we spent our youth watching our father transform homes with precision and care. He taught us that a great paint job isn't just about the finish—it's about the preparation, the integrity of the materials, and the respect shown to a client's space.

Five years ago, we took that legacy and started our own independent operation. We combined the old-school techniques we learned from the best with modern tools and a fresh perspective on service. Today, NextGen Painting is the result of that journey—a business built on family values and professional excellence.
