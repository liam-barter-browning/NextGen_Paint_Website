NextGen Painting

NextGen Painting is a Prince Edward Island-based business founded by two brothers with a passion for high-quality finishes and professional service. While we officially launched the NextGen brand a year ago, we have been painting professionally since 2013, bringing over a decade of hands-on experience to every project. We specialize in transforming spaces through expert interior and exterior painting, kitchen cabinet refinishing, and specialized wood staining.

Owner-Operated Excellence: When you hire NextGen, you're working directly with us. We take pride in our preparation and attention to detail, ensuring that every home or business we touch receives a first-class finish that lasts.
